1. Introduction

Welcome to WhatsApp Expense Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp number for authentication and messaging
  • Email Address: For communication and account recovery
  • Expense Data: Amounts, descriptions, categories, and timestamps of expenses you record
  • Budget Settings: Categories and budgets you configure

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • WhatsApp ID: Unique identifier for your WhatsApp account
  • Usage Data: Dates and times of interactions with the service
  • Technical Information: Basic data necessary for service operation

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the expense tracking service
  • Send you budget updates and reports
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the service and develop new features
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • All data is stored in secure databases on Google Cloud Platform
  • We use encryption for sensitive data storage
  • Access to information is limited to authorized personnel only

4.2 Retention Period

  • Expense data is retained while your account is active
  • After account closure, data is retained for 30 days for possible recovery
  • After that, all data is permanently deleted

5. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • WhatsApp Business API: For sending messages (WhatsApp/Meta processes the messages)
  • Infrastructure Providers: Google Cloud Platform for service hosting
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order

We will never sell your information to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Information: Request a copy of all information we have about you
  • Correct Information: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete Information: Request deletion of all your information
  • Data Export: Receive your data in an accessible format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us via WhatsApp or email.

7. Use of Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Language Preference: To save your language choice (Hebrew/English)
  • Session ID: To manage your login

All these cookies are essential for service operation and are not used for tracking or marketing.

8. Information Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data encryption at rest
  • Limited database access
  • Ongoing security monitoring and auditing
  • Secure backups

9. Children's Privacy

The service is intended for ages 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to delete it.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via WhatsApp message or email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, you can contact:

  • Via the WhatsApp bot
  • By email (details to be provided)

12. Privacy Law Compliance

This policy complies with the Israeli Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981 and the Privacy Protection (Data Security) Regulations, 5777-2017.
